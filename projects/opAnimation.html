Short Opening Animation with Parallax

Idea

For this project, I wanted to create an opening sequence for a documentary that explores the journey of the products we consume until they reach our tables, either at home or in a restaurant. My inspiration comes from a McDonald's commercial released this year. However, the idea is to use five images to tell the story without text, rather than selecting images related to a specific theme.

In the first image, I would showcase a vegetable in a field, perhaps with a rain effect. Another image would depict the food being transported. Following that, an image of this food being cooked (possibly with a smoke effect) would be presented. Subsequently, I would show how it is plated, and finally, someone enjoying the meal. Perhaps at this point, with camera movement, the documentary's title could be introduced, possibly mirroring the title of the commercial.

Image edition

I separated the different elements of the images with Photoshop and added the layers of the removed elements

Video edition

In After effects I tried different camera movements to finally find the movements that I finally used. And added some effects like smoke, light and fire to the animation to be more dynamic.
